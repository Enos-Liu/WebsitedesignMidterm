Sources of recommendation

This website is specially designed for the fans of Disney. There are many branches of the website. This is the one which mainly recommend cartoons. Those who like to watch Disney cartoons can sign up! If one is registered, other sub-sites can be logged in directly. Those who are interested in Disney can sign up now. I hope all of you can have a pleasant experience!

Disney cartoons are very classic, and our website will be updated according to the number of new movies played. You're not going to pull down any good work. Babies also can send email(DisneyCartoonRecommendations@outlook.com) to our website for movie reviews. Everyone is welcome to give their own ideas, so that our website can be better and better. Thank you for your participation!

When no babes send us reviews, we take comments from the comments section of various websites. We decide very carefully before we put a movie that's really worth watching on the website. It includes comments sent from people whose views we gathered before we launched the site. A combination of film experts and Disney people.
